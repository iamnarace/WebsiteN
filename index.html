◆ FAQ

Common questions

What does "No Fix, No Fee" actually mean?+
If I diagnose a software or hardware issue and it turns out to be genuinely unfixable, you pay nothing for the diagnostic work. You only ever pay when there's a real, working result.
Do I have to sign a contract or monthly plan?+
Never. Everything is 100% ad-hoc and pay-as-you-go. No retainers, no lock-ins, no minimum spend. You reach out when you need help, and that's it.
Which areas of Sydney do you cover?+
Greater Sydney, with a focus on Western Sydney and the Parramatta region. Many issues can also be resolved remotely, wherever you are in Sydney.
Can you help outside business hours?+
Yes. I offer evening and weekend availability specifically to minimise downtime for busy households and small businesses.
Do you work with both homes and businesses?+
Absolutely. From family Wi-Fi and smart home setups to Microsoft 365 and Intune device management for small teams — the same engineering precision applies either way.
Can issues be fixed remotely?+
Many software, Microsoft 365, and configuration issues can be resolved quickly and securely over the internet using remote desktop tools — often same-day and at a lower cost than an on-site visit.
